My Favorite Movies

I love movies that tell emotional, creative, and powerful stories. Korean films like Train to Busan and Parasite amaze me with their depth and emotion, while others like Avengers: Endgame and Avatar inspire me with incredible visuals and action. These films make me laugh, cry, and think — that’s why they’re my favorites.

Train to Busan

A thrilling zombie film about love, survival, and sacrifice on a moving train.

Parasite

A darkly comic look at class divisions and the struggle between rich and poor families.

Exhuma

A mysterious Korean horror film about exorcism, spirits, and ancient secrets.

Forgotten

A gripping mystery about memory loss and shocking family secrets.

The Handmaiden

A beautifully crafted psychological drama filled with deception and desire.

The Call

A tense Korean thriller where two women connect through a phone across time.

Black Panther

A Marvel superhero story celebrating African culture and leadership.

Aladdin

A magical Disney story about freedom, love, and believing in yourself.

Zootopia

A fun animated film about teamwork, friendship, and equality.

Avatar

An epic sci-fi adventure about connection, nature, and courage.

Elemental

A touching Pixar story about love and acceptance between fire and water.

Avengers: Endgame

The ultimate Marvel superhero movie about hope, teamwork, and sacrifice.
