  2. After reading the articles, I realized how much help framing a project can help . When framing a project in a story format, it helps refine ideas more clearly and easier to follow and mix mistakes. The framing can be done by breaking it down in narrative structure, roles, motivation, conflicts and resolution. When putting a project into a story structure, you need to identify who the user is, what issue or problems they conflict with, how the design solves that issue or problem and finally what the outcome is. This helps to see what the main parts are, what can be fixed if there are any and make it more refined.
