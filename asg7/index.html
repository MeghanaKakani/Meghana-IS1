Domain Name Discussion

Possible Domain Names

  • stellarodyssey.org
  • stellarhelp.net
  • thestellarhelp.com

Classmate Discussion

My classmate and I discussed ideas for our website name. We both liked the word “stellar” because it sounds bright, positive, and space-themed. We also thought the players could be called “Stellars,” which makes the name feel more personal and community-driven.

Chosen Domain

I chose stellarodyssey.org as my favorite because it feels adventurous and inspiring. It fits our theme of exploring new ideas together as a group of Stellars.
