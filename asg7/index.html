Leadership. Compassion. A future for all systems.

Short Bio

Leia Organa of Alderaan is a seasoned stateswoman, diplomat, and leader who helped found the New Republic and has spent her life fighting for democracy and human dignity across the galaxy. A former senator and resistance leader, Leia combines strategic courage with empathy — a rare blend that has earned her respect from allies and opponents alike.

Why she would make a good ruler

Leia's experience in building institutions from the ground up and her commitment to protecting civil liberties make her uniquely qualified to lead. She values coalition-building over coercion, understands the burdens of leadership, and consistently centers the needs of ordinary citizens in policy decisions. Her calm under pressure and refusal to compromise on justice would guide the nation toward a more stable, inclusive future.

Learn more on her Wikipedia page: Leia Organa — Wikipedia.

Running Mate

Han Solo

A pragmatic and resourceful partner, Han Solo brings real-world negotiation skills, quick decision-making, and broad popular appeal. His background as a smuggler-turned-hero gives him a unique perspective on reforming trade, transport, and veterans' services. Together, Leia and Han balance principled leadership with practical problem-solving.

Top Campaign Platform

The campaign will focus on three core pillars: (1) Restoring and strengthening representative institutions so citizens across all systems have a voice; (2) Comprehensive security reform that prioritizes de-escalation, community safety, and transparent oversight of defense resources; and (3) A bold economic recovery program that invests in infrastructure, equitable education access, and interstellar trade policies that uplift working families. Leia's platform emphasizes diplomacy over domination, fairness over favoritism, and long-term resilience over short-term gain.
