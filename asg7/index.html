Short Bio

Kim Namjoon, known globally as RM, is the leader of BTS — a world-renowned South Korean music group. Beyond his musical achievements, RM is recognized for his intelligence, eloquence, and leadership both on and off stage. Fluent in English and deeply passionate about art, literature, and philosophy, RM represents a new generation of thoughtful, socially aware leaders who bridge creativity with compassion.

Why he would make a good ruler

RM has consistently shown a deep sense of empathy and wisdom. His speeches — from the United Nations to award ceremonies — highlight his belief in self-love, mutual respect, and the power of youth to change the world. His ability to bring people together through words and action demonstrates the calm, intelligent leadership that a future president should embody.

Learn more on his Wikipedia page: RM — Wikipedia.

Running Mate

Hongjoong (ATEEZ)

Hongjoong, the leader of ATEEZ, is known for his creativity, passion, and advocacy for self-expression. As RM’s running mate, he would bring a fresh, innovative approach to leadership — inspiring people to think boldly and embrace individuality. Together, RM and Hongjoong form a powerful duo of intellect, artistry, and purpose — uniting generations under a shared vision of empathy and progress.

Top Campaign Platform

RM’s campaign focuses on three pillars: education, creativity, and unity. His goal is to create a society where art and learning go hand in hand — where education values creativity as much as academics. He aims to promote mental health awareness, sustainability, and cross-cultural collaboration, ensuring that every voice — regardless of age, gender, or background — is heard and valued. RM believes that leadership starts with understanding and that together, we can build a future that balances logic and love.
