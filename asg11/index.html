The Demon Hunter Team

When darkness rises, only the bravest stand between our world and the abyss. Meet my elite Demon Hunter Team — each warrior forged by fire, armed with power, and ready to strike down evil wherever it lurks.

Tanjiro Kamado

With his Water Breathing techniques and unshakable heart, Tanjiro brings balance and courage. His determination is unmatched — once he sets his sights on a demon, it’s over.

Inosuke Hashibira

Wild and fearless, Inosuke charges headfirst into danger. His beastly instincts make him unpredictable in combat and impossible to outmaneuver.

Nezuko Kamado

A demon with a human soul, Nezuko’s strength and speed are incredible. Her Blood Demon Art lets her burn through evil itself, protecting her teammates at any cost.

Geralt of Rivia

The White Wolf — a seasoned monster slayer armed with silver blades and Witcher magic. Geralt brings experience and cold precision to the hunt.

Dante

Half-demon, half-human, all style. Dante’s dual pistols and massive sword make him a one-man army. He turns every battle into a show — and demons never stand a chance.
