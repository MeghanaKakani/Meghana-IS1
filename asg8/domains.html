Website Domain Name Discussion

Possible Domain Names

  • stellarodyssey.org
  • stellarhelp.net
  • thestellarhelp.com

Classmate Discussion Summary

My classmate and I discussed possible names for our website. We wanted something inspiring and space-themed, so we focused on the word “stellar.” We came up with the idea of calling our users “Stellars,” since it fits the theme and makes them feel like part of a community. We both liked how this idea made the project sound more creative and inviting.

Chosen Domain Name

Out of the three, I chose stellarodyssey.org as my favorite. It sounds adventurous and fits the purpose of our project. The word “odyssey” gives a sense of discovery and progress, which connects perfectly with our audience of Stellars.
