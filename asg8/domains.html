Classmate Discussion

My classmate and I discussed possible names for our website. We liked the name “stellar” because it sounds bright and space-related. We also decided to call the players “Stellars,” which makes the project feel more connected and friendly.

Chosen Domain

My favorite name is stellarodyssey.org because it feels inspiring and creative. It fits the idea of exploration and teamwork among our Stellars.
