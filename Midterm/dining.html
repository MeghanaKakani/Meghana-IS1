Delicious Options

Jaedong Sundubu

This place serves a seriously amazing bowl of sundubu jjiggae. Jaedong Sundubu is one of the most famous sundubu (soft tofu) restaurants in Korea. Its sundubu jjigae is loved by many for its fresh ingredients and rich, flavorful broth. The soft and creamy tofu pairs perfectly with the spicy yet savory soup, creating a delicious balance, especially when eaten with rice. In addition, Jaedong Sundubu offers carefully prepared side dishes and a clean, welcoming atmosphere, making the dining experience very comfortable. That’s why so many people describe it as “the essence of Korean sundubu” and consider it incredibly delicious.

Bukchon Son Mandu

Bukchon Son Mandu is famous for its handmade Korean dumplings. Each mandu is freshly made with care, filled with juicy, flavorful ingredients that melt in your mouth. The wrappers are tender yet slightly chewy, perfectly holding the savory fillings. Whether steamed, boiled, or pan-fried, the dumplings are always delicious and satisfying. Combined with warm, traditional side dishes, eating at Bukchon Son Mandu is a comforting and authentic Korean culinary experience that keeps people coming back for more.

Hanchu

Hanchu is famous for its perfectly crispy Korean fried chicken and flavorful sauces. Each piece of chicken is fried to golden perfection, maintaining a juicy and tender inside while being crunchy on the outside. The sauces—ranging from sweet and spicy to savory soy garlic—add a burst of flavor with every bite. Paired with cold beer or traditional sides, eating at Hanchu is a fun, satisfying experience that captures the best of Korean fried chicken culture.
