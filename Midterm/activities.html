Top Activities in South Korea

Visit Gyeongbokgung Palace

Step back in time and explore one of Seoul’s most iconic palaces. Witness traditional architecture, historical artifacts, and the famous changing of the guard ceremony. You can rent out traditional outfit for the day. Go around enjoying the how it was like in the old days.

Climb Namsan Tower

Enjoy panoramic views of Seoul from Namsan Tower. A favorite spot for couples to hang “love locks” and for tourists to admire the cityscape. It has one of the most becautiful views to sit and relax with your friends or by yourself.

Relax on Jeju Island

Escape the city hustle and unwind on the beautiful beaches of Jeju Island. Explore lava tubes, waterfalls, and scenic coastal trails for the perfect getaway. The way to get to jeju island is also any amazing journy. You can go by car drive or there is a fari that takes you to the island.
