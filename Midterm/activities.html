Top Activities in South Korea

Visit Gyeongbokgung Palace

Step back in time and explore one of Seoul’s most iconic palaces. Witness traditional architecture, historical artifacts, and the famous changing of the guard ceremony.

Climb Namsan Tower

Enjoy panoramic views of Seoul from Namsan Tower. A favorite spot for couples to hang “love locks” and for tourists to admire the cityscape.

Relax on Jeju Island

Escape the city hustle and unwind on the beautiful beaches of Jeju Island. Explore lava tubes, waterfalls, and scenic coastal trails for the perfect getaway.
